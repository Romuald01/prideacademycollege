About our School

About School Campus

Greensprings School opened its Lekki Campus in 2005, this was specifically built as a fit-for-school purpose to meet the ever-changing needs of 21st century education. The campus has over 1400 pupils from pre-school age through to IB Diploma students (2 yrs to 18 yrs). Great boarding facilities cater for approximately 400 students – mostly of secondary school age – in a caring environment, complete with 24 hours operational health clinic within the Lekki campus. Greensprings School Lekki campus is located along the Lekki / Epe expressway, close to the Lekki free trade zone and the new Dangote refinery, the Lekki campus offers-spacious and learning friendly classrooms, well equipped science laboratories, specially designed rooms for art, music and life science; to ensure a well-rounded education for the students, Lekki campus also has the following in-place for sports and recreational activities:- swimming pools, athletics track, basketball courts, football pitches and tennis courts, all set in a safe and secure school environment.

About US

Pride Academy College is an award winning institution, where future leaders are groomed. We offers first-class education from the foundation stage to post-secondary levels. Our offers include Crèche, Preschool, Elementary School, Secondary School and Sixth Form.
